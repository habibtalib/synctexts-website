## The Challenge

KAFA (Kelas Al-Quran dan Fardhu Ain) Islamic primary education across Selangor involves many teachers, classes, and administrative processes managed by Jabatan Agama Islam Selangor (JAIS). The department needed a single online system to manage teachers and operations.

## Our Approach

We built e-GKAFA on Laravel with a FilamentPHP back office and role-aware portals:

- **Officer (Pegawai) portal** for administration, records, and oversight.
- **Teacher (Guru) portal** for teacher-facing tasks and submissions.
- **Multi-panel architecture** with language and panel switching.
- **Records management** for KAFA teachers, classes, and supporting data.

## Results

- A centralized system for managing KAFA teachers and operations.
- Clear separation between administrative and teacher experiences.
- A maintainable admin interface for day-to-day departmental use.
