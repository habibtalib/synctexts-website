## The Challenge

Jabatan Laut Malaysia (the Marine Department) manages large volumes of maritime operational data under the national e-Navigation initiative. The department needed a secure, centralised platform to manage, monitor, and maintain this data reliably.

## Our Approach

We support and operate the Maritime Data Management (MDM) platform on the eNav domain:

- **Secure access** — role-based account login for department officers and administrators.
- **Real-time updates** — live data push to dashboards via SignalR.
- **Operational data management** — DataTables-driven admin screens over the department's maritime records.
- **Resilient data layer** — MongoDB with replication for high availability.
- **Ongoing modernisation** — a Laravel-based v2 rebuild is in progress alongside continued operation of the current system.

## Results

- A dependable central platform for the Marine Department's maritime data.
- Real-time visibility for operational users.
- A clear modernisation path without disrupting day-to-day operations.
