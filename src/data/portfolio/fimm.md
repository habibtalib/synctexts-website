## The Challenge

The Federation of Investment Managers Malaysia (FIMM) regulates and supports investment-management professionals and needed a web platform to serve its members and support compliance processes.

## Our Approach

We delivered a web platform supporting FIMM's member and compliance operations:

- **Member services** through a dedicated portal.
- **Compliance processes** digitised for members and administrators.
- **Secure access** behind an enterprise web gateway.

## Results

- A central online channel for FIMM members.
- Digitised compliance workflows.
- A maintainable platform for the federation's ongoing needs.
