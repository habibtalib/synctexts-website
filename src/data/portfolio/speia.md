## The Challenge

Monitoring Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) projects involves coordinating state investigators, environmental officers, site visits, and a constant stream of structured reports — across many environmental media and compliance guidelines. The department needed one system to manage it all with a reliable audit trail.

## Our Approach

We built SPEIA, a Laravel platform with a Vue.js front end and OAuth via Laravel Passport:

- **Project & investigation management** for EIA projects, assigning state investigators and environmental officers.
- **Environmental media monitoring** across air, water, river, land, and lake sampling categories, plus waste management tracking.
- **Reporting engine** for monthly reports, investigation reports, and scoring — with extensive PDF and Excel generation (DomPDF, Snappy, mPDF, TCPDF, PhpWord, Laravel Excel, JpGraph).
- **Governance** through role-based permissions, activity logging, model auditing, and scheduled backups.

## Results

- A unified compliance workspace replacing scattered manual reporting.
- Consistent, exportable reports for internal review and enforcement.
- Full auditability of every record change across the EIA lifecycle.
