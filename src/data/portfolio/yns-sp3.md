## The Challenge

Yayasan Negeri Sembilan needed a single online system to handle student education-financing — from application intake through approval, disbursement, and repayment — replacing manual and fragmented processes.

## Our Approach

We delivered SP3, a Laravel 12 platform with a FilamentPHP admin back office:

- **Applicant onboarding & application** with an online portal and "Apply Now" flow.
- **Loan processing** workflows for review, approval, and disbursement.
- **Repayment tracking** and account management for borrowers.
- **Operational tooling** including Livewire datatables, printable documents, queueable bulk actions, language and panel switching, and full activity logging.

## Results

- Centralized education-loan operations for the foundation in one auditable system.
- Self-service applicant portal reducing manual paperwork.
- Maintainable admin interface that non-technical staff can operate day to day.
