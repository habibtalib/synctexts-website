## The Challenge

The Malaysia Air Traffic Controllers Association (MATCA) — the professional body representing the nation's air traffic controllers, affiliated with IFATCA — needed an official online home: a place to recruit members, publish association activities, and represent the profession publicly under its motto, "For the Nation and Profession."

## Our Approach

We delivered the association's official portal on WordPress with Elementor:

- **Membership recruitment** with a prominent "Join MATCA" flow for prospective members.
- **Events showcase** — Merdeka Day rides, family camps, futsal and golf tournaments, mental-health workshops (Critical Incident Stress Management), and international engagements such as IFATCA APRM and the AEROTHAI technical visit.
- **Gallery and merchandise** sections for the association community.
- **Editorial-friendly CMS** so the association's exco could publish updates themselves.

## Results

- A professional public presence for Malaysia's air traffic controller community.
- A self-service platform the association managed independently.
- The portal has since been retired; this capture is preserved via the Internet Archive.
