## The Challenge

The Selangor Land & Mines Office (Pejabat Tanah dan Galian) manages permits for rock and quarry materials (bahan batuan). Processing applications, approvals, and renewals manually was slow for both officers and permit holders.

## Our Approach

We delivered Smart Docket, an online permit system for rock-material licensing:

- **Online permit applications** for rock-material (bahan batuan) permits.
- **Approval & renewal workflows** for new applications, annual approvals, and monthly permit renewals.
- **Permit-holder portal** with online submission and status tracking.

## Results

- Digitised rock-material permit processing for the state.
- Faster applications and renewals for permit holders.
- A central, trackable record of permits and approvals.
