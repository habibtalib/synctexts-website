## The Challenge

The Merdeka Times needed an online publishing platform to deliver news to readers — with editorial publishing, organised categories, and a fast public-facing portal.

## Our Approach

We delivered an online news and media platform:

- **Article publishing** with an editorial workflow.
- **Categorised content** for easy reader navigation.
- **Reader-facing portal** optimised for browsing the latest news.

## Results

- A dedicated home for The Merdeka Times' reporting online.
- Organised, browsable news for readers.
- A maintainable platform for ongoing publishing.
