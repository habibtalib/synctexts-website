## The Challenge

The Noir beauty brand grows through a network of agents and entrepreneurs ("Usahawan Noir"). They needed a platform to onboard members, manage agents, and run day-to-day sales operations online.

## Our Approach

We built the Usahawan Noir platform as an agent and member management system:

- **Member onboarding & registration** with a branded public landing and sign-up flow.
- **Agent dashboards** for managing accounts, orders, and performance.
- **Sales operations** supporting the brand's direct-sales agent network.

## Results

- A central platform for the Noir agent and entrepreneur network.
- A polished, on-brand experience for prospective and existing members.
- A reusable foundation consistent with SyncTexts' other agent systems.
