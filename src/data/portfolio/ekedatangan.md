## The Challenge

Institut Koperasi Malaysia (IKMa) needed a reliable, computerised way to capture and manage staff attendance, replacing manual processes with a system tied to biometric check-ins.

## Our Approach

We delivered eKedatangan, a Laravel 12 attendance platform:

- **Biometric attendance capture** with computerised records.
- **Leave and calendar tracking** using an interactive FullCalendar view.
- **Reporting** for attendance summaries and staff records.
- **Role-based access** for staff and administrators.

## Results

- Accurate, centralized attendance records for the institute.
- A clear calendar view of attendance and leave.
- A maintainable system replacing manual attendance tracking.
