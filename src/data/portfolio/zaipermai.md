## The Challenge

ZaiPermai operates through a network of agents and needed a member system to onboard agents, track sales and commissions, and manage day-to-day member operations.

## Our Approach

We built a Laravel-based agent and member management platform:

- **Agent onboarding** and member account management.
- **Sales & commission tracking** across the agent network.
- **Member operations** and a dedicated member login portal.

## Results

- A central platform for managing the ZaiPermai agent network.
- Streamlined sales and commission visibility.
- A reusable foundation for multi-brand agent systems.
