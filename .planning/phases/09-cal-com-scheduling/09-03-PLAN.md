Start the dev server with `npm run dev` and present the following verification checklist to the user. Open http://localhost:4321/contact in the browser.

**What was built:**
Cal.com inline booking embed in the contact form success panel with dark theme, indigo accent, name/email prefill, lazy loading, and a webhook endpoint that links bookings to lead records in SQLite.

**Pre-requisite:** Ensure `CAL_WEBHOOK_SECRET` is set in `.env` and the Cal.com webhook is configured in the Cal.com dashboard pointing to your domain's `/api/cal-webhook`.

**Verification checklist:**

CAL-01 -- Embed renders after form submission:
- Navigate to /contact
- Complete the multi-step form (select a service, fill details, submit)
- After "Message Sent!" appears, verify the Cal.com booking calendar loads below the success message
- Verify a loading spinner appears briefly while the embed loads

CAL-02 -- Dark theme and indigo accent:
- The Cal.com embed should have a dark background (not white)
- Interactive elements (buttons, highlights) should use indigo (#6366f1) accent color
- The embed should feel visually consistent with the glassmorphism design

CAL-03 -- Prefill from form data:
- When the Cal.com embed loads, click on an available time slot
- The booking form should show your name and email prefilled from the contact form submission

CAL-05 -- View Transitions:
- After seeing the success panel with Cal.com embed, navigate to another page (e.g., click "Home" in nav)
- Navigate back to /contact
- Verify: the form shows fresh/empty (not the success panel), no console errors
- Submit the form again and verify the Cal.com embed loads correctly a second time

CAL-04 -- Webhook lead linking:
- Complete an actual booking through the Cal.com embed (or use Cal.com's test/sandbox mode)
- Check the SQLite database: the lead record matching the booking email should have `cal_booking_uid` and `cal_scheduled_at` populated
- Command: `sqlite3 data/submissions.db "SELECT id, email, cal_booking_uid, cal_scheduled_at FROM submissions ORDER BY id DESC LIMIT 5;"`

Fallback behavior:
- If Cal.com embed fails to load (e.g., block the CDN in browser DevTools), verify a "Book a Discovery Call" fallback link appears after ~10 seconds

Prompt user: Type "approved" or describe specific issues found.
